GET HELP

Please read the FAQs below first.

If you don't find the answer to your questions, visit the support forum


Q. How will I be notified of courses that are applicable to my role?

You will receive an email communication about the availability of a course if one has been developed for your role. As the course library grows, you will find more and more courses that are open for your registration.


Q. Will I be able to discover open edX courses on Infopedia?

Yes, the Open edX catalog will be available in Infopedia.


Q. Will my grades show in my Infopedia transcript?

Yes.


Q. Will my manager see the courses I’ve taken in InfoPedia?

Yes.


Q. What is the minimum percentage required to pass the course?

Each course can have a unique grading policy. Review the About page for the course to understand the requirement to pass the course.


Q. How do I track my course progress?

Open your course and select the Progress tab


Q. How do I register?

To register, click launch on a course card on Infopedia. You will be registered and enrolled directly from Infopedia.


Q. I’d like to try the Open edX experience, but no courses are available to me right now.

The best way to get a sense of the user experience is by taking the edX Demo course. You can also find over 100 courses delivered by Microsoft that are open to the public on https://www.edx.org/school/microsoft.

Q. What do I do if I am having trouble viewing a video?

Please click on the download video button located below the video player. See screenshot.
